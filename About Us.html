We Are Adventurers

We share our passion for exploring abandoned places. We are a group of adventurers who have a deep appreciation for the history, mystery, and beauty of abandoned places. Our goal is to inspire others to explore the world around them and discover the hidden gems that are often overlooked.

Our Belief in the Fascination of Abandoned Places

At Urbex America, we believe that there is something truly fascinating about abandoned places. Whether it's an old factory, an abandoned hospital, or a forgotten city, these forgotten places offer a glimpse into the past and the opportunity to witness the beauty of decay.

We understand that for many people, exploring abandoned places is a thrilling and adventurous activity, and we are here to support and celebrate that passion.

Safety First: Our Commitment to Safe Urban Exploration

Our team is made up of experienced urban explorers who are dedicated to providing valuable information and resources for those who want to explore the hidden corners of America. We know that exploring abandoned places can be dangerous, and we take safety very seriously.

That's why we provide tips and guidelines for safe urban exploration, as well as recommendations for gear and equipment to ensure that explorers are fully prepared for their adventures.

Join Our Community of Urban Explorers Today

Urbex America is also a community for like-minded individuals to connect and share their experiences. We encourage our members to share their photos, stories, and tips with the community, and we strive to foster a welcoming and supportive environment for all.

Whether you are an experienced urban explorer or just starting out, Urbex America is the perfect place to explore, learn, and connect with other enthusiasts. Join us today and start exploring the hidden corners of America!
