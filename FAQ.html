Frequently Asked Questions

You ask the questions. We provide the answers.

General

Q: What is urban exploration?

A: Urban exploration, or urbex, is the exploration of abandoned or off-limits urban locations such as buildings, tunnels, and rooftops.

Q: Is urban exploration legal?

A: The legality of urban exploration varies by location and circumstance. It is important to research and understand the laws and risks associated with urban exploration before embarking on any explorations.

Q: How do I stay safe while urban exploring?

A: It is important to always prioritize safety while urban exploring. This includes wearing appropriate clothing and gear, researching the location beforehand, and never exploring alone. Our safety guide page provides more detailed tips and guidelines for safe urban exploration.
