Abandoned Places In Texas

Uncover the abandoned sites of Texas

Name: House in the Desert

Location: El Paso, TX

Cordinates: 31.670580, -106.009912

Status: Active

In the middle of the vast desert landscape of El Paso, Texas stands an abandoned house that has been charred and burned. The origins of this once-stately structure are shrouded in mystery, with no clear information about who built it or why it was left to decay. The only thing that remains is the burned shell of the house, its empty windows staring out into the unforgiving desert wilderness. The desolate surroundings and the haunting remains of this house make for an eerie and intriguing sight, attracting urban explorers and adventurers from all over who seek to uncover the secrets of its past.

Name: Cold Storage Plant

Location: Harligen, TX

Cordinates: 26.2009947, -97.7022971

Status: Active

The abandoned cold plant in Harlingen, TX, is a haunting reminder of the town's industrial past. The building is now covered in vibrant graffiti, adding a surreal and almost dreamlike quality to the scene. Inside, you'll find rows of retro computers, dusty machinery, and other industrial relics from a bygone era. The plant's history is shrouded in mystery, with locals only able to speculate about its origins and the reasons behind its abandonment. Despite its dilapidated state, the plant continues to draw in urban explorers and photographers, drawn by its eerie beauty and the sense of history that seems to emanate from its walls.

Name: The Forgotten Orphange/House

Location: El Paso, TX

Cordinates: 31.725619, -106.205042

Status: Active

The abandoned orphanage in El Paso, TX is a haunting reminder of the city's past. While not much is known about the building's origin, it is believed to have been used as an orphanage many years ago. The eerie atmosphere of the place is accentuated by the graffiti that adorns its walls, providing a unique and striking contrast against the old, weathered brick. The bullet shells and beer bottles scattered around the area are a testament to the fact that the place is frequented by visitors, despite its abandoned state. The building debris and rubble only add to the sense of mystery and intrigue surrounding the site, making it a popular destination for urban explorers and thrill-seekers alike.
