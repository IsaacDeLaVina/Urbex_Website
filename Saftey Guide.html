Saftey Guide

Exploring abandoned places can be an exciting and rewarding experience,
but it can also be dangerous if proper safety precautions are not taken. Here are some safety tips and guidelines to help ensure a safe and enjoyable exploration

Research and Plan Ahead

Please understand that urban exploring is illegal and considerly dangerous, so do your own diligence to research the area and gather as much information as possible about the location.

This includes the historical records, current state of the area, and any potential safety hazards. Create a plan for your visit and share it with somone you trust.

Bring Appropriate Gear and Equipment

Wear appropriate clothing and footwear for the location and conditions, and bring a first aid kit, flashlight, and any other necessary equipment. Consider bringing a mask and gloves for protection against potential hazards such as dust and debris.

Always seek permission

Trespassing is illegal and can result in fines or even arrest. Always seek permission from the property owner or relevant authorities before entering any abandoned site.

Be Aware of Hazards

Abandoned sites can be unstable and hazardous. Be aware of potential hazards such as weak flooring, falling debris, and exposed nails or wires. Use caution when exploring and always watch your step.

Never explore alone

Always explore with at least one other person. This ensures that there is someone to assist in case of an emergency or injury, and also provides a source of support and companionship.

Explore Solo at Your Own Risk

If you decide to explore alone, please be aware that it is at your own risk. It is important to take extra precautions to ensure your safety, such as being mindful of your surroundings and bringing necessary equipment in case of an emergency.

Respect the site

When exploring abandoned places, it's important to respect the site and leave it as you found it. Avoid damaging or removing any items or artifacts, and take care not to disturb any wildlife or plants.

Be mindful of your surroundings

Pay attention to your surroundings and be aware of any potential dangers. If you feel unsafe or uncomfortable at any point, it's okay to leave and explore another site.

Remember, safety should always be your top priority when exploring abandoned places. By following these guidelines and using common sense, you can enjoy a safe and rewarding exploration of these forgotten and fascinating locations.
